About my work

Paintings

Most of my paintings are created with my large assortment of acrylic paints. I have experimented with different types of paints in the past, such as oils and watercolors, and while I enjoyed those, I found acrylics to be my favorite kind to work with. Its quick-drying properties and vibrant colors make it easy to create small details and portray colorful scenes in a beatiful way.

Drawings

My drawings are made up of many different materials. I love to experiment with various drawing tools and the ones I have found myself to use most are Prismacolor Colored Pencils, regular graphite pencils, and ink pens. The material I enjoy working with most for drawings is my set of Prismacolor pencils. Their extremely pigmented color is perfect for creating vibrant, detailed drawings.

Body Art

Body art is, in my opinion, definitely one of the most "out there" forms of art. I mess around with body art as a way to keep my styles interesting and to express my creativity. For body art, I either do something as simple as draw on a friend with an ink pen, or take out a facepaint palette and go to town. When I use facepaint, I use a Wolfe Face Art palette because the paints in this set are extremely pigmented and do not become diluted as easily.

Digital Art

For digital art, I stick with programs such as Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. These applications make graphic design fun and interesting and have a wide variety of tools to help you create whatever designs you could think up.
